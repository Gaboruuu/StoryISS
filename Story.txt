This story has been written by Doru, Gaboruu, Mihai and Razvan of team Ubb Frat Leaders.

You and your friends decide to become adventurers, so you go to the adventurers guild to register as a party.
Your party is called Ubb Frat Leaders, and is formed from 4 members.
Each member has a different class.
- Razvan is a tank.
- Doru is a swordsman.
- Mihai is a mage.
- Gaboruu is a spy.

You decide to start your story of your first mission.
Your party has Rank C.
You head to the quest board to choose a mission.
Your options are:
1. Defeat 10 Slimes.
- reward: 10 bronze coins
- difficulty: Rank C
- location: Slime Valley

2. Collect herbs
- reward: 5 bronze coins
- difficulty: Rank D
- location: Fairy Forest
