This story has been written by Doru, Gaboruu, Mihai and Razvan of team Ubb Frat Leaders.

You and your friends decide to become adventurers, so you go to the adventurers guild to register as a party.
Your party is called Ubb Frat Leaders, and is formed from 4 members.
Each member has a different class.
- Razvan is a tank.
- Doru is a swordsman.
- Mihai is a mage.
- Gaboruu is a spy.

You decide to start your story of your first mission.
Your party has Rank C.
You head to the quest board to choose a mission.
Your options are:
1. Defeat 10 Slimes.
- reward: 10 bronze coins
- difficulty: Rank C
- location: Slime Valley

2. Collect herbs
- reward: 5 bronze coins
- difficulty: Rank D
- location: Fairy Forest

Your party chooses "Defeat 10 Slimes".

You and your party head to the Slime Valley.
After 20 minutes, you arrive at the target location.
You and your party divide into 2 teams to kill slimes faster: Gaboruu and Doru, Mihai and Razvan.
After a while, you manage to defeat the 10 slimes and collect the slime essence to prove your completion.
While collecting the essence, Razvan spots a bunch of monsters in the distance.
Gaboruu, the spy, uses his stealth skill to scout around the area.
After coming back, he tells his team that 10 goblins are camping in the nearby area, protecting a treasure.
After a while, you decide to vote on your next action:
1. Retreat and claim the prize
2. Clear the camp and claim the treasure.
